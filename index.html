End-to-End Intelligence Flow

From physical footfall to digital insight in milliseconds.

1. The "Smart Mat"

Located at the Drop-off zone. A pressure-sensitive mat applies a micro-thin RFID sticker to the shoe sole in 0.4 seconds. No stopping, no batteries.

2. The Mesh Network

As the passenger walks, the tag "pings" off existing WiFi Access Points and discrete floor sensors. This creates a continuous dot on the Ops Dashboard.

3. The Peel-Off

At the Arrival Exit, a textured floor strip (similar to an escalator comb) gently detaches the tag. A vacuum system collects it for 100% recycling.
